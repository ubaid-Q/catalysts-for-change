      About US

      About

      Empowering Communities and Creating Sustainable Impact

      The Catalysts For Change Foundation is a charitable, not-for-profit organization incorporated [Under section 16 of the Companies Act, 2017 (XIX of 2017)] registered in ‘’Security & Exchange Commission of Pakistan -SECP’’ is based in Karachi, Pakistan’. We are a humanitarian development org. specially work for the most vulnerable areas to support people to break the cycle of marginalization and poverty through creating opportunities and building self-reliance. Our interventions are aligned with the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) to achieve impact at scale on the SDGs, eradicate poverty, quality education, Safe drinking water, reduce inequalities, climate change, environmental degradation, and major collective challenges and to unite efforts and build resilient, inclusive communities for all. We adhere to the humanitarian principles of impartiality and neutrality and implement activities across all geographies in Pakistan from the most vulnerable remote areas to developed urban centers. We promote empowerment to vulnerable groups, and support their efforts to be aware of their basic rights, responsibilities, and aspirations especially women/girls,persons with disabilities and transgenders influence changes that are environmentally, socially and institutionally, sustainable.
